## RECOMMENDED PRIORITY FOR TRAINING

### Tier 1 - Direct Ataturk content (scrape/download first)
1. Nutuk full text (Turkish + English) from Internet Archive
2. Wikipedia articles (all languages) via API/dumps
3. Wikiquote + Wikisource texts
4. Quotes from all aggregators

### Tier 2 - Biographical/historical context
5. Books from Archive.org (Kinross, Mango, etc.)
6. Project Gutenberg Turkish texts
7. Academic papers from CyberLeninka (free full text)
8. ATAM Research Center publications

### Tier 3 - Turkish language general corpus
9. trwiki-67 dataset (67M words, ready to use)
10. umarigan/turkish_corpus on HuggingFace (34 GB)
11. TS Corpus (1.8B tokens)
12. TTC4900 categorized news

### Tier 4 - Massive scale (if needed)
13. OSCAR Turkish subset
14. CC-100 Turkish
15. OPUS parallel corpora
